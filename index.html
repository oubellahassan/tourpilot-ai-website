Finance & Invoicing

Know your margins in real time — not after the trip

Stop guessing your profitability. TourPilot AI calculates gross margins, net margins, and markup as you build itineraries. Generate branded invoices and payment reminders in seconds — synced to Xero automatically.

  • Live margin calculator as you add services to itineraries
  • Multi-currency support with automatic exchange rates
  • One-click Xero synchronization for invoices and credit notes
  • Payment tracking dashboard with automated overdue alerts
💡 The result: You'll spot unprofitable bookings before they happen, not after. Finance moves from a bottleneck to a competitive advantage.

  • Bulk import/export with CSV and Excel compatibility
  • Supplier performance tracking and booking history
  • AI-powered fuzzy matching for automatic supplier resolution
💡 The result: Building a quote used to require checking 5 different spreadsheets. Now your team finds the right supplier and price in seconds.
